Who I Am

Technology Leader.
Business Builder.

Olufunso Festus Tokede is the Executive Director and IT Manager of Funshy Audio Visual Limited — a technology and audiovisual solutions company headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria. With a career built on hands-on technical depth and executive leadership, he bridges the gap between complex technology and practical business outcomes.

His expertise spans hospitality IT, network engineering, AV systems integration, enterprise infrastructure, and digital transformation. Beyond technology, Olufunso is an entrepreneur actively expanding Funshy Audio Visual Limited's footprint across Nigeria and into international markets — driven by a mission to honor God, serve others with integrity, and build businesses that last.

He combines technical precision with strategic thinking, applying AI tools and modern digital systems to accelerate growth and solve real-world problems for clients and communities.
